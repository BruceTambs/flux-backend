Poses
Version 0.2 — March 2026

----------------------------------------

Purpose
    Define the character’s body position, action, and camera orientation for each generated image.
    Poses are style‑agnostic and identity‑agnostic.

----------------------------------------

Orientation Options
    All poses support the following camera orientations unless otherwise noted:
        0° (Front)
        45° to Camera
        90° (Profile)
        180° (Back)
        45° Back to Camera

Defaults
    If pose is unspecified:
        Use Standing — Neutral.
    If orientation is unspecified:
        Use 0° (Front).
    If expression is unspecified:
        Use neutral or friendly.
    If camera angle is unspecified:
        Use straight‑on, eye‑level framing.

----------------------------------------

Rules
    Poses must not alter character identity.
    Poses must not override attire unless explicitly stated.
    Poses should remain physically plausible unless the chosen style requires stylization.
    Group poses must maintain consistent character proportions.
    Orientation variants apply to all poses unless otherwise noted.
