EmotionPresets
Version 0.1 — March 2026

----------------------------------------

Purpose
    Define the character’s facial expression in a consistent, reusable way.
    Emotions are style-agnostic and do not alter character identity.
    Emotions may be combined with any pose, background, or orientation.

----------------------------------------

----------------------------------------

Defaults
    If emotion is unspecified:
        Use Neutral.
    If multiple emotions are selected:
        Use the first listed unless otherwise instructed.

----------------------------------------

Rules
    Emotions must not alter character identity.
    Emotions must remain physically plausible for the chosen pose.
    Emotions should not override style or lighting.
    Emotions may be combined with any pose or background.
    Emotions should remain appropriate to professional or instructional contexts unless explicitly requested otherwise.
